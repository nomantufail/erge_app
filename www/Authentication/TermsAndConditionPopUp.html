2. Overview.

Erge operates for the benefit of people who would like to have items delivered for them (“Service Requestors” or “Requestors”), and for the benefit of those who would like to participate by delivering these items (“Service Providers” or “Providers”). To summarize, the Service generally operates as follows:

  • A Requestor uses the Service to post a request for services the Requestor would like performed (a “Task”).

  • Erge users may search for or be notified by the Service that the Task is available.

  • A Provider accepts the Task posted by the Requestor, and the Requestor agrees to let the Provider perform the Task.

  • The Provider performs the Task, which generally entails obtaining an item or items requested in the Task for the Requestor.

  • The Provider then presents the Requestor with the item or items.

  • The Requestor pays Erge for the Task. A portion of this amount is retained by Erge, the remainder is paid to the Provider.

  • The Requestor and the Provider may rate each other and offer feedback which is then shared with other users.
